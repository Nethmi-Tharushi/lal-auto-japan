Our Mission

At Lal Auto Japan, our mission is to deliver both brand-new and high-quality pre-owned vehicles to customers across Sri Lanka — with honesty, transparency and care.

We make vehicle ownership simple by providing clear vehicle histories, independent inspections, and straightforward pricing. Whether you choose a brand-new model or a certified used car, we aim to give you confidence in your purchase.

We support customers through every step — from test drives and trade-ins to financing and after-sales advice — building trust long after the keys are handed over.

What We Offer

We stock a carefully chosen selection of brand-new and used vehicles, including compact cars, family sedans, SUVs and select premium models. Our services include:

  • Official dealership and sourcing of selected brand-new models.
  • Certified pre-owned cars with multi-point inspections and verified documentation.
  • Trade-in valuations and part-exchange options.
  • Flexible financing plans and assistance with registration paperwork.
  • After-sales support, warranty options and trusted service referrals.

Each car is prepared for sale and listed with accurate photos, specs and an honest condition report so you can compare options and make an informed decision.

Why Choose Us

We combine the benefits of brand-new vehicles (reliability, warranty) with the value of well-maintained used cars — all under one roof. Our customers choose us for:

  • Transparency — clear histories, honest inspections and fair pricing.
  • Support — help with financing, test drives and paperwork.
  • Quality — every vehicle is checked and prepared for sale.
  • Local knowledge — experienced team who know the Sri Lankan market.

We pride ourselves on friendly, reliable service and long-term relationships. When you buy from Lal Auto Japan, you’re buying with confidence.
