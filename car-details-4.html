Vehicle Description

The Suzuki Alto is a compact kei-class city car engineered for exceptional fuel economy, easy urban manoeuvring and low running costs. Offered in grades A, L, Hybrid S and Hybrid X, the Alto provides simple, reliable transportation with modern conveniences and safety features in higher grades. The 660 cc engine (with hybrid assistance on the Hybrid grades) makes it ideal for daily commuting and narrow streets while keeping maintenance and ownership costs low.
